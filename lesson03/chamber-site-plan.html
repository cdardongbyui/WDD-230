Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to support Guatemalan entrepreneurs to develop the potential of their companies. It is a site aimed at all types of entrepreneurs, from small, medium and large companies. Providing for entrepreneurs and their companies: business advice, foreign trade, providing a job bank, in addition to supporting them to generate projects that add value to their companies, training them in all aspects and types of initiatives.
